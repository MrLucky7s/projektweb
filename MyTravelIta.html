Italy

Italy, a European country with a long Mediterranean coastline, has left a powerful mark on Western culture and cuisine. It's capital, Rome, is home to the Vatican as well as landmark art and ancient ruins.

Aside from the beautiful landscape, Italy's major cities include Florence, with Renaissance masterpieces such as Michelangelo’s "David" and Brunelleschi's Duomo; Venice, the city of canals; and Milan, Italy’s fashion capital.

Italy provides an exploration of culture, activity, local cuisine and art.
